About This Project

NIFTY 50 Stock Price Prediction

This project uses machine learning to predict future NIFTY 50 index values. We trained our models on 10 years of historical data (2016-2026) using Facebook's Prophet library, which is a time series forecasting tool that handles trends, seasonality, and holidays.

How It Works
  • Historical NIFTY 50 data is preprocessed and enriched with technical indicators like moving averages (5, 20, 50 day) and volatility.
  • Four separate Prophet models are trained to predict Open, High, Low, and Close prices.
  • The models capture weekly and yearly seasonality patterns along with the overall market trend.
  • Users can input any future date and get predicted OHLC values.
